About Web Klinic

Kyle is an aspiring front-end developer and graphic designer with a passion for uplifting the informal business sector. Having studied Multimedia at Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT), he specializes in helping individuals and businesses from lower-income areas bring their ideas to life through affordable digital solutions. At Web Klinic, his mission is to empower those with great ideas but limited resources, providing high-quality services to help them thrive in the digital space.
